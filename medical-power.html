TO MY PHYSICIAN AND MY FAMILY

This declaration is made by me of ID No. , Nairobi in the Republic of Kenya at a time when I am of sound mind, and after careful consideration.
If the time comes when I can no longer take part in decisions on my own future, then let this declaration stand as the testament to my wishes:
I appoint of ID No. and of ID No. to take care and be in charge of my health and wealth fare needs.
If it is the opinion of two (2) independent doctors that there is no reasonable prospect of my recovery from physical illness, or from impairment expected to cause me severe distress or to render me incapable of rational existence, then I request that and have the final say of if I should be allowed to die and not be kept alive by artificial means. I also request that I be treated with whatever quantity of drugs or medicines that may be required to keep me free from pain or distress, regardless of the effect on my physical health and survival.
